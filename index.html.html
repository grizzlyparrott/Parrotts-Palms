About Us

At Parrott’s Palm and Tree Services L.L.C., we specialize in palm trimming, tree care, and full yard maintenance for residential and commercial clients across Mohave Valley and surrounding areas. With a focus on safety, precision, and professionalism, we treat every property like it’s our own — keeping your palms healthy and your yard clean year-round.
